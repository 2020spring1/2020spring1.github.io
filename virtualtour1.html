History of France

Early Modern France

This period of France took place from around 1453 to 1789, and was ruled by the house of Valois and later the house of Bourbon.

It is remembered for being one of the first versions of France, and one of the most powerful versions, as well as for one of its kings, who is considered the greatest absolute monarch of all time.
