History of France

Revolutionary France

This period of France took place from around 1799 to 1815, after Napoleon gained power and became the first emperor of the First French Empire.

It is remembered for being a period of French dominance, where Napoleon took over the majority of Europe before he was defeated and exiled for the rest of his life.
