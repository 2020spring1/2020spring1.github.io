History of France

Revolutionary France

This period of France took place from around 1789 to 1799, after the people overthrew the Monarchy is support of a new Republican government.

It is remembered for being one of the leading factors to many of the European revolutions in the years after, as well as the Reign of Terror, where countless French lives were taken through execution at the guillotine.
