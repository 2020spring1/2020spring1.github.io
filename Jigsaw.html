Russia - Jigsaw Activity

*This activity is for those who wish to learn more about the different periods of Russia*

In this activity you will work in a group of five students. Each of you will become an "expert" at one of the different periods of Russia. After becoming an expert, you will present and teach your group about your phase of Russia.

Directions

  1. Get into groups of three people.
  2. Each group member will pick a random number from 1 to 3. This number is the number of your expert group.
  3. Use the table below to find the period of Russia that goes along with your expert group number.
  4. Use the resource links to study your period of Russia. When you have finished reading, use the practice link to make sure you have become proficient in this type of factoring.
  5. Next, join a new group composed of the other "experts" with your same number/type of factoring. Discuss the phase of Russia with your expert group and answer any questions.
  6. Lastly, return to your original class groups. Each student will get a turn to teach their method of factoring to their class group. Make sure to include examples and to have your class group practice these examples.
  7. Lastly, return to your original class groups, where you will present your phase of Russia to the class.
  8. Once all groups are finished, the class will have a Russia quiz composed of all 3 phases of Russia.
